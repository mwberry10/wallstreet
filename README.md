wallstreet tracker

This will allow you to "invest" fake money into the market and see the theoretical gains/losses.

The data is ~15 minutes behind.

This will be a website that one has to login to. Once logged in, the user can allocate themselves "money" to "invest" in the stock market. They will see certain data about how much money they would have gained or lost (if their money had been real).
